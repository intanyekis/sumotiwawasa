    Devaughn Jones Obituary, Death – An SUV struck a motorcycle on Wednesday at a junction in Gwinnett County, according to the authorities. The biker was shot and died. The collision, which took place at approximately 7:30 p.m., resulted in the death of Devaughn Jones, who was 24 years old and from Lawrenceville.

    As stated in a police report that was acquired by AJC.com, Jones was travelling eastward on Georgia State Route 120 while riding his 2008 Honda CBR 600. Samantha Dickson, 37 years old, was driving her 2014 Kia Sportage in the direction of the west. The report states that Dickson came to a stop at the intersection leading to Lakes Parkway and was waiting to make a left turn prior to the intersection.

    As the sun began to drop, she reported to the cops that there was a glare, but she did not see any cars approaching. According to the investigation, Dickson began to make a left turn when she unexpectedly saw Jones’ motorbike directly in front of her. As a result of the collision between her SUV and the motorcycle, Jones was ejected from the motorcycle, according to the incident report.

    Following the collision, both the automobile and the motorcycle were rendered inoperable and were subsequently removed by a wrecker agency. In a press release issued on Friday, the Gwinnett County Police Department spokeswoman, Cpl. Michele Pihera, stated that it does not appear that alcohol impairment played a role in this crash. The investigation into the matter is still ongoing, and it is possible that charges will be brought at a later date.
