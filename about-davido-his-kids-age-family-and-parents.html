    What do we know about Davido?

    Davido, whose birth name is David Adedeji Adeleke, was born on November 21, 1992. He is a Nigerian-American singer, songwriter, and record producer, recognized for popularizing the Afrobeats genre globally. Born into affluence in Atlanta, Georgia, Davido moved to Lagos, Nigeria, at the age of two, where he began his journey into music during his high school years. In 2012, he released his first album, “Omo Baba Olowo,” which garnered significant commercial success and established his prominence in Nigeria.

    Since then, Davido has released several successful albums and has been honored with numerous awards, including the BET Award for Best International Act in 2018 and the MTV Africa Music Award for Best Male Artist in 2019. His music, known for its infectious tunes, vibrant rhythms, and dynamic stage presence, resonates with a wide audience. Additionally, he has collaborated with international artists such as Drake, Nicki Minaj, and Chris Brown.

    Davido’s Family and Kids

    Davido, an accomplished singer, is a father of four. In 2015, his daughter Aurora Imade Adeleke was born to him and Sophia Momodu. Two years later, in 2017, he welcomed another daughter, Hailey Veronica Adeleke, with a woman named Amanda. Subsequently, in 2019, his son David Ifeanyi Adedeji Adeleke Jr. was born to him and Chioma Rowland.

    Davido, now 30 years old, was born to Adedeji Adeleke, a prominent Nigerian business figure, and Vero Adeleke, a university lecturer.
