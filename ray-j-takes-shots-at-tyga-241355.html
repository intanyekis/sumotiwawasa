    Well, this came out of nowhere.

    For reasons unknown, Ray J took a shot at Tyga earlier this week. Asked by TMZ for his two cents regarding rumors surrounding the Young Money rapper and Kylie Jenner, Kim Kardashian’s ex was rather harsh with his reply.

    “My girl like Blac Chyna, so Tyga need to get back with Blac Chyna; we f–k with her. He need to stop f–king with little kids. If he wanna fuck with a Kardashian, he need to do it the right way.”

    In Tyga’s VIBE digital cover story, the rapper addressed the lack of positive role models for young black women, pointing to VH1’s Love & Hip-Hop (which Ray J currently stars on) as a misguided source of inspiration.

    “I don’t even watch VH1, ‘cause it’s poison. A lot of women watch that stuff and it just takes them off the focus of what they think happiness is,” he said.
