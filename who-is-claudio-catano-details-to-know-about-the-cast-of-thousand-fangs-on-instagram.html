    Who Is Claudio Cataño? 

    Claudio Cataño is a professional actor, director, and screenwriter. 

    The actor has mostly appeared in television series in his acting career, including Black widow 2, Maria Magdalena, The Inquisitor, and The Chosen Granddaughter.

    Besides that, his cinema credit includes Time out, Peaceful, and Nobody Knows Who She Works For. 

    Claudio Cataño Age And Wikipedia 

    Claudio Cataño is presently 34 years old, according to Wikipedia. 

    He was born on September 18, 1985, in Bogotá, Colombia. 

    Claudio began studying acting at the Rubén Di Petro School at sixteen years old. When he was 17 years old, Cataño won his first audition, and he has been working ever since.

    Claudio has collaborated with some of Colombia’s most talented filmmakers.

    In 2008, he made his debut in the audiovisual realm with one of his first starring parts in the television series The Sky Branch.

    His filmmaking career began in 2012 with the short films Canalst and APT101, in which he combined his passion for anime, artistic pondering, and hundreds of cinematographic references. 

    Similarly, he took part in the National Theater of Colombia’s season with the piece La forma de las cosas in 2014.

    Furthermore, he created the script for Moria, his debut feature picture as a filmmaker, in the same year.

    Who Is Claudio Cataño Wife?

    Claudio Cataño is now married to his wife, Patricia Castañeda Giraldo. 

    Patricia is also an actress who has been active in the film industry for over twenty-nine years.

    She is 43 years old as born on February 16, 1978. Giraldo has many television series, movies, and theatre work under her credit. 

    Patricia got nominated for best actress by the Colombian Film Academy at the Macondo Awards in 2016 after Claudio encouraged her to play in Moria.

    Besides acting, Claudio’s wife, Patricia, is also a novel writer. Her novel, Virginia Casta, which won the Best Novel prize at the Latino Book Awards in NYC, has been produced into a film.
