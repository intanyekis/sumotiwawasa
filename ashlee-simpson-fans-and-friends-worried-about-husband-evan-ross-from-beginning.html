    Ashlee Simpson was once one of Hollywood's most controversial figures. First, she was known as the reclusive younger sister of singer and reality TV star Jessica Simpson. Then, she emerged from the shadows to become a successful musician before her career was ruined after she was caught lip-singing during a Saturday Night Live performance in 2004.

    Simpson also had to deal with the multiple controversies surrounding her father and former manager, Joe Simpson. She also went through a heartbreaking and highly publicized divorce with the father of her first child and former husband, rocker Pete Wentz.

    Ashlee Simpson's public persona and personal life turned around after she married Evan Ross in 2014. And while the former singer and actress appears happy in her relationship, there were people who voiced their concerns about Ross in the beginning of their courtship.

    Evan Ross Admitted That Friends Of Ashlee Simpson Warned Her About Him

    Ashlee Simpson and actor Evan Ross began dating in 2013 after meeting at a mutual friend's birthday party.

    As fans know, Ross is an actor and one of five children born to legendary musician Diana Ross and her second husband, Norwegian business executive Arne Naess Jr.

    When the couple first met, Ashlee Simpson had been divorced from her husband, Pete Wentz, for nearly two years.

    Ross and Simpson later married at Diana Ross' home in Connecticut in 2014; the couple welcomed a daughter named Snow in 2015 and a son named Ziggy in 2020.

    While Simpson remains largely out of the limelight, she and her husband share multiple successful businesses and occasionally create music together.

    In an interview on the Drew Barrymore show in March 2021, Evan Ross stated that not long after he met Ashlee Simpson, he knew she was the one he wanted to spend the rest of his life with and added, "Marrying her was the best decision I ever made."

    Ashlee Simpson added, "Marriage takes work and understanding each other, and it's so important to stay on point and grow with one another. I'm very lucky to have Evan as my life partner. He's amazing."

    While there is no denying that Simpson and Ross have a lot of love for one another, fans and friends of the two haven't always loved their union. Evan Ross once shockingly admitted that a close friend of Ashlee Simpson's warned her that he was bad news.

    The surprising admission was revealed when Evan Ross was a guest on the Not So Hollywood Podcast in 2023.

    During one particular program segment, Evan Ross opened up about the early years of his relationship with his wife, which included a story about how a friend disapproved of them dating.

    The incident occurred when the couple first met at their mutual friend's party. At one point, Ross stated that he and Simpson danced together; he later added:

    I actually just came from a recording studio... I was like, 'Hey, you should come with me.' We listened to music, we made out. I think her friend Stephanie said I was trouble or something like that...So it ended up ending there for a little bit.

    Later in the program, Evan Ross said after some time apart, he decided to rekindle his relationship with Simpson.

    Evan Ross Revealed That He Is Friends With Wife Ashlee Simpson's Exe's

    Although not everyone was initially thrilled with Ashlee Simpson's relationship with Evan Ross, the two have since forged close bonds with other people.

    For example, Evan Ross revealed he's remained close friends with a few of Ashlee Simpson's exes.

    The surprising admission was revealed during a joint interview with Ashlee Simpson and Evan Ross on the Watch What Happens Live Show with host Andy Cohen.

    The couple were there to promote their 2018 reality show Ashley + Evan; the show aired on the E! Network for one season before it was canceled due to low ratings.

    During one portion of the show, a caller asked the couple if they felt doing a reality TV series would jeopardize their relationship.

    Ashlee Simpson responded: "No, we don't. At first, we thought it would be more like a docu-series. And then we were like, your relationship is what your relationship is. We don't think a show can make or break us."

    Evan Ross added, "I don't think it's possible to ruin a relationship because of anything you do. It all depends on whether or not you're supposed to be together."

    Andy Cohen revealed later in the show that Evan Ross was good friends with Ashlee Simpson's ex-boyfriend, singer Ryan Cabrera. Cohen then asked Ross about his relationship with Simpson's ex-husband and the father of her first child, Pete Wentz.

    Evan Ross replied: "Very good, he is such an amazing guy and father. I grew up in a family with a lot of different siblings from different marriages. So I have a big family. And I think it is a beautiful thing. My relationship with Pete is amazing."

    Ross continued, "As I said, he's an amazing person and father. He knows that I love his son very much, but that I respect their father and son relationship. I think we have such a great friendship and many great experiences with each other. It's a beautiful thing."
