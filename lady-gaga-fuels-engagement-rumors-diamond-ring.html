    Lady Gaga Sparks Engagement Rumors

    Lady Gaga and Michael Polansky have been dating for over 4 years now, and it seems they are still going strong. They first announced their relationship in February 2020, with Gaga making an Instagram post that showed the two of them cuddling on a boat.

    While they have largely kept their relationship private, only occasionally sharing updates on their feelings, they never gave any indication of their plans for the future. In fact, a couple of years ago, a source shared that the couple wasn't planning on getting married any time soon, as the pop star was focusing on her career.

    "They haven't been talking about engagement or marriage yet," the source said back in 2021. "Friends don't think that will happen any time soon. She's very busy with her career and is not thinking about long-term plans right now, but she loves having him by her side. She was in a great mood at the inauguration and happy that Michael was there with her for that experience."

    But that was then. Now, 3 years later, things might have changed. Lady Gaga was spotted walking around West Hollywood, and fans were shocked to see a huge diamond ring on her finger, looking very much like an engagement ring.
