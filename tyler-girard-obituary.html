    Tyler Nicole Girard, aged 27, of Breaux Bridge, was killed on Tuesday, 25th October 2022, after failing to stop at a stop sign on Bourque Road, which resulted in a pickup truck running over her. 

    KPEL News covered the incident on Tuesday. She was thrown into a shed on private land by that hit.

    The family is mourning the death of Tyler Girard. 

    Tyler Girard’s Obituary and funeral details are not revealed yet. 

    The Accident That Took Her Life

    On Monday night, just before 8:00 p.m., that crash occurred. 

    A crash in St. Martin Parish resulted in the collision of another young woman only a few hours later, at around 2:00 a.m. 

    Rylee A. Georgeson, aged 20, of Denham Springs, according to Louisiana State Police, was struck close east of an abandoned Kia car that was discovered in the middle of the westbound lanes of I-10 west of Breaux Bridge near mile marker 108.

    After a pickup truck allegedly struck Georgeson and stopped on the inside shoulder, she was found on the other side of the eastbound lanes close to the fog line, according to the police. 

    She was the only resident of that abandoned Kia and owned it. Georgeson was struck after exiting the Kia; that vehicle was never involved in the collision.

    Georgeson was taken to the hospital in severe condition and later passed away. Results from an analysis of a toxicological sample are still waiting.

    Ben Latiolais, 26, of Arnaudville, who was the driver, was tested for intoxication in the meantime. 

    According to State Police, his breath sample showed that he was not intoxicated, and is currently being held for his first DWI charge in the St. Martin Parish Jail. The inquiry is ongoing.

    Tributes Were Paid Following Her Death

    A source close to her posted, ‘This morning’s news was not what we wanted to hear. The world will be darker now that this lovely soul has received her angel wings. Tyler Girard had the infectious smile and laugh that we needed.’

    ‘Sweet girl, take it easy. We will sincerely miss you. Love and prayers are being sent to the Girard family.’

    We respect the family’s privacy and cannot reveal Tyler Girard’s cause of death because a statement will not be made public until then.
