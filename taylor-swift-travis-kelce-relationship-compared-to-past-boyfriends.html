    Many would likely say that no one had a more exciting 2023 than Taylor Swift both professionally and personally. While she kept setting records in her music career, like with her amazing Eras tour, she is in a happy relationship with Travis Kelce. Ever since rumors started up about Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift dating in September 2023, the world has been watching this sweet romance.

    While Taylor Swift has dated many celebrities, her relationship with Travis Kelce feels unique for many reasons, and fans enjoy following along with every football game and date night. Here's why the singer's current romance stands out.

    6 Instead Of Being Private, Taylor Swift Talks About Her Love Life Now

    Taylor Swift's Most Recent Ex Is Matty Healy

    There is always speculation about Taylor Swift's romantic life, with fans wondering if one particular song is about her ex Joe Alwyn. Now that Swift is dating Travis Kelce, she seems more willing to share her opinions about the negative things people say about who she's in a relationship with. Before, she didn't seem to want to draw attention to it.

    Swift said:

    "By the time I went to that first game, we were a couple. I think some people think that they saw our first date at that game? We would never be psychotic enough to hard launch a first date."

    5 While Taylor Swift's Exes Mostly Stayed Silent, Travis Kelce Speaks About Her Often

    Travis Kelce Co-Hosts The Podcast New Heights With His Brother Jason Kelce

    When Taylor Swift was interviewed by TIME Magazine, she called her romance with Travis Kelce "public." Ever since people began noticing that the two were hanging out, Kelce has been very open about the fact that he's dating the beloved singer. In contrast, Swift's ex-boyfriends didn't talk to the media or share their feelings on a podcast. Fans only found out details about the relationship once Swift and her partner had broken up.

    According to People, Kelce has talked a lot about Swift. Whether he's calling her "smart" or praising her concert in Argentina, he sounds in awe of her. Kelce also addressed the fact that she's a huge celebrity:

    "The scrutiny she gets, how much she has a magnifying glass on her, every single day, paparazzi outside her house, outside every restaurant she goes to, after every flight she gets off, and she’s just living, enjoying life. When she acts like that I better not be the one acting all strange.”

    3 Travis Kelce Is Taylor Swift's First Serious Boyfriend Not In The Entertainment Industry

    Travis Kelce Is Number 87 And A Tight End For The Kansas City Chiefs
