    Easily one of the most successful comedy actors of her generation, at times it has seemed like Julia Louis-Dreyfus has the Midas touch. After all, unlike the vast majority of actors who’ve starred in an iconic show, Louis-Dreyfus has starred in more than one sitcom that will go down in history as an all-time great. In fact, she played such a key role in the success of her latest show that its production came to a complete halt when she couldn’t appear on set through no fault of her own.

    Once you examine Julia Louis-Dreyfus’ career as a whole, it makes sense that she is a very wealthy person. However, even some of her most loyal fans have no idea that other members of Julia’s family have been big breadwinners, too.

    Julia Louis-Dreyfus' Husband

    Julia Louis-Dreyfus' husband Brad Hall, is worth $10 million

    Whenever Julia Louis-Dreyfus and her husband Brad Hall appear at a red-carpet event, there is no doubt that she is the one who commands almost all the attention. Despite that, Brad Hall actually has accomplished a lot more in the entertainment industry than most people in the business, which is why his net worth is $10 million.

    After Julia Louis-Dreyfus and Brad Hall met and fell in love at Northwestern University, they both were hired to be a part of Saturday Night Live in the early-‘80s. While most SNL fans seem to have completely forgotten Brad Hall was a part of the show, he landed one of the most coveted gigs of all when he became a Weekend Update anchor. For that reason, Hall has something in common with some of the most beloved SNL stars of all time.

    Brad Hall has also created shows like The Single Guy and he was a writer, producer and director on the show Brooklyn Bridge.

    Julia Louis-Dreyfus' Family Business

    Julia Louis-Dreyfus' family business, the Louis Dreyfus Company is worth $120 billion

    Long before Julia Louis-Dreyfus was born, her great-great-grandfather Léopold Louis-Dreyfus founded the Louis Dreyfus Company. While most people have never heard of it, the Louis Dreyfus Company’s annual gross sales have exceeded $120 billion.

    Since Léopold Louis-Dreyfus passed away in 1915, his fortune and interests in the company he founded have been passed down in Julia’s family. For example, before Julia’s father Gérard Louis-Dreyfus passed away in 2016, he chaired the Louis Dreyfus Company.

    As a result of the company's success, Gérard reportedly was able to amass a $4 billion net worth before he passed away which was inherited by Julia, her siblings, and her step-mother. For that reason, it has been suggested that Julia Louis-Dreyfus is likely to become a billionaire in the future.

    Julia Louis-Dreyfus' Incredible Career

    Julia Louis-Dreyfus' acting career has made her a $250 million fortune

    Julia Louis-Dreyfus has a current net worth of $250 million, which makes her one of the wealthiest Seinfeld stars. While it is clear that Brad Hall and Louis-Dreyfus’ forebearers have contributed a lot of money to her family’s fortune, she has made a big chunk of her family’s fortune.

    Still best known as one of the stars of the legendary sitcom Seinfeld, Julia Louis-Dreyfus played a massive role in that show’s years of success. For proof of this, all you have to do is tune into an episode and observe how hilarious Julia was at every turn. Alternatively, you could watch Seinfeld’s pilot in which Elaine Benes doesn’t appear since her character was created after that episode was filmed. Unsurprisingly, that episode simply doesn’t work the same way due to Louis-Dreyfus’ absence.

    After Seinfeld came to an end, Michael Richards and Jason Alexander tried to find new sitcoms to star in but fans didn’t accept them in their new roles. While that doesn’t say anything bad about those two actors, Julia Louis-Dreyfus’s fans have remained loyal as she has found new roles. For example, from 2006 until 2010 she starred in The New Adventures of Old Christine and the show enjoyed a great deal of success.

    Once The New Adventures of Old Christine came to an end, Julia Louis-Dreyfus hit gold once again when she agreed to star in Veep. Easily among the most acclaimed shows of the last decade, Veep was a huge hit with audiences and viewers alike.
