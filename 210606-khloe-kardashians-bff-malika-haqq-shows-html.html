    Malika Haqq, the best friend of reality television star, Khloe Kardashian, of "Keeping Up With The Kardashians" fame, shared a new picture on her Instagram page featuring her baby son, Ace Flores.

    Khloe Kardashian's BFF, Malika Haqq, can't get over her baby son, Ace Flores, which is understandable because the 2-month-old is a bundle of cuteness.

    In the new picture, which the proud mom captioned with a black heart emoji, Ace wore a black onesie and sat on a couch, staring at the camera. On his little feet was a wooly footwear that featured the colors blue, white, and purple.

    Malika shares her son with her ex, O.T. Genasis. The cute baby was born in March, and since then, his mother and father have been making several posts about their son.

    Malika and O.T. Genasis are doting parents, and the rapper also showed that side of him when he shared an adorable video of himself talking to baby Ace on his Instagram, captioning it:

    "Conversations #Daddygang, I love you son."

    In the video, the rapper asked his son, "What's up," with the baby doing his best to answer his father's question with sounds of his own. Genasis then said he missed his baby, before adding, "You're my boy."

    Cute Ace gave his father all of his attention throughout the entire video, and many of Genasis's fans commented on the post, with one of them saying the video will make for a beautiful memory to show Ace in the future.

    Since giving birth to Ace, Malika Haqq has taken motherhood like a professional and revealed that she is looking forward to having more kids.

    It didn't take long for Malika to get back to shape after her baby's delivery, and she showed how quickly she recovered when she shared a sultry picture of herself on social media in April.

    The 35-year old shared a picture of herself donning a black T-shirt bra that showed her ample cleavage. She accessorized her beautiful look with a crucifix necklace and diamond earrings.

    Malika's make up was natural and perfect, and she styled her brunette hair in loose waves. She looked to be resting on a bed with white blankets in the picture, though there was no sign of her baby son.

    The actress has filled her Instagram with many posts of herself and baby Ace. She also recently shared a lovely picture that showed her holding her newborn and looking at his face with so much love while sleeping in her arms.

    The actress, who became famous for being the best friend to Khloe Kardashian and making appearances on "KUWTK" along with her twin sister, Khadijah, captioned the post by writing, "My world."
